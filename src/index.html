Before You Begin

Best Practices

Memoro Vault is not a wallet. It is a last-resort backup for long-term storage of sensitive data. If you are protecting seed phrases or private keys, only decrypt them in Tails OS for maximum security.
Avoid using your real name in the questions or when naming your vault. Initials only.
The vault’s strength comes from cryptography, but blending in as a plain .zip file increases your safety. Don’t draw attention to it.
Choose answers that outsiders cannot easily guess. Avoid obvious or common choices.
Always start from a clean session. Restart your computer or boot fresh into Tails OS before use.
Do not paste decrypted text (like seed phrases) into other apps. Clipboard history can leak sensitive data.
Memoro Vault includes a “nuke” function that clears traces when you exit:
  • Clipboard contents
  • Browser storage (IndexedDB, localStorage, sessionStorage)
  • Temporary decrypted files
This reduces leftovers, but remember: malware or keyloggers can still capture data live. Make sure your internet is turned off before decryption.
Only use the official Memoro Vault program. Avoid modified or unofficial versions. Always verify hashes before installation.
